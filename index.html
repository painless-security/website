About

Find Out More About Us

Painless Security has been providing security consulting and software development services for over 15 years, largely for the Research & Education and non-profic sectors.

What we do.

We specialize in Trust & Identity solutions for Research & Education, developing and operating Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) and Infrstructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) solutions on various cloud platforms, Internet technologies, and Embedded Systems development.

We also offer Collaborate, a web-based Software-as-a-Service product that offers fully-hosted solutions to connect to global R&E Trust and Identity services, eduroam and eduGain.

Team

Our Hardworking Team

Margaret Cullen

Managing Partner

Margaret Cullen is the President & CEO of Painless Security. Margaret has over 30 years of experience in all aspects of software development. She formerly served as VP of Engineering at ThingMagic, Director of Networks at WindRiver, and Director of Technical Services at FTP Software.

Margaret is a globally recognized expert on Internet Security and IPv6. She currently serves as Chair of the Internet2 Community Architecture Committee for Trust and Identity (CACTI), and as Chair of the IETF RADIUS Extensions (radext) Working Group. She formerly served on the (U.S.) Presidents' Critical Infrastructure Protection Board, as an IETF Internet Area Director, and as Co-Chair of the IETF IPv6 (ipv6) and IPv6 Operations (v6ops) Working Groups.

Mark Donnelly

Senior Software Developer

Mark Donnelly is a security consultant and software developer at Painless Security. He also contributes to the IETF. He has written and co-written several Internet-Drafts on topics such as detecting captive portals, checking RADIUS server status, and improving usability for federated authentication.
